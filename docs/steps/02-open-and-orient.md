# Step 2: Open Your New Repository and Orient Yourself

## Goal
Make sure you are working inside your own copy and understand the key files.

## Instructions
1. Open your new repository on GitHub.
2. Review `README.md`, `index.html`, and `style.css`.
3. Open the **Code** tab and confirm your files are present.

## Checklist
- [ ] I opened my own repository.
- [ ] I found the starter files.
- [ ] I know where `README.md`, `index.html`, and `style.css` are.
